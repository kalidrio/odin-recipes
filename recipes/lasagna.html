Lasagna

The perfect lasagna recipe made with parmesan ricotta cheese filling, melted mozzarella cheese ...

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion (finely diced)
  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Start by making a homemade meat sauce.
  2. Make a parmesan ricotta cheese mixture.
  3. Use your favorite lasagna noodles.
  4. Bring on the cheese!
  5. Bake covered for 15 minutes
